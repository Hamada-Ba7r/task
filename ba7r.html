Leonardo Wilhelm DiCaprio

Leonardo Wilhelm DiCaprio is an American actor, film producer, and environmental activist. He is known for his work in biopics and period films, and has won numerous awards, including an Oscar, a BAFTA Award, and three Golden Globe Awards.

Willard Carroll Smith

Willard Carroll Smith Jr. is an American actor, rapper, film producer, songwriter, and writer born on September 25, 1968. He has enjoyed success in television, films, and songs. Tom Cruise
